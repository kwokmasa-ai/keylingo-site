Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

KeyLingo ("we", "our", "us") operates the KeyLingo multilingual keyboard application for iOS. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Keyboard data and full access

KeyLingo requires "Allow Full Access" to enable translation and language-switching features. This permission is required by iOS for any keyboard that communicates with external services. Here is exactly what we do and do not do with this access:

What we access:

  • Text you type is processed to provide translation and language suggestions
  • Translation requests are sent to our translation service to convert text between languages

What we do NOT do:

  • We do not store, log, or retain any text you type
  • We do not collect passwords, credit card numbers, or other sensitive input
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties for advertising
  • We do not track your keystrokes or build typing profiles
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data on your device

Information we collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the app:

  • Account information: If you create an account, we store your email address for authentication purposes
  • Subscription data: Purchase and subscription status is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We receive anonymized subscription events (e.g., trial started, subscription renewed) but do not have access to your payment details
  • Usage analytics: We may collect anonymized, aggregated data such as which languages are most popular, to improve the app. This data cannot identify individual users
  • Device information: iOS version and device model for compatibility and crash reporting

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Handles payment processing for subscriptions. Apple Privacy Policy

Data retention

Translation requests are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers. Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Data security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transmitted between the app and our servers. Translation requests are processed over encrypted connections and are not stored after processing.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Export your data in a portable format

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
