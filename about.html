Language should never be a barrier

KeyLingo was built for the 1.5 billion people who speak more than one language — and the billions more who want to connect across languages.

Our story

Growing up between cultures means growing up between languages. You text your mom in one language, your friends in another, your coworkers in a third. But your keyboard only speaks one at a time.

We built KeyLingo because we were tired of the copy-paste dance — type a message, switch to Google Translate, paste, copy the translation, switch back, paste again. Every single time.

KeyLingo puts every language right where you need it: in your keyboard. Type in English, send in Korean. Switch to Spanish mid-sentence. Translate a message you received without leaving the chat. One keyboard, every language, zero friction.

Who it's for

KeyLingo is for anyone who lives between languages. The Korean-American who wants to text their grandmother in Korean. The expat in Paris who needs to email in French. The K-pop fan who wants to tweet in Hangul. The immigrant who doesn't want to lose their mother tongue.

If you've ever wished your keyboard understood you the way you think — in more than one language — KeyLingo is for you.

Our commitment

Your privacy comes first. We don't store what you type, we don't sell your data, and we never will. KeyLingo processes translations in real-time and forgets everything the moment your message is sent. Your conversations are yours alone.
