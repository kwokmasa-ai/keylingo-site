About KeyLingo

KeyLingo is a multilingual keyboard for iOS that lets users type and translate across 100+ languages without leaving their conversation. Designed for the 1.5 billion people who speak more than one language, KeyLingo eliminates the copy-paste cycle between messaging apps and translation tools. Users type in their preferred language and KeyLingo translates in real-time before sending — working seamlessly in iMessage, WhatsApp, Instagram, and every other app. The keyboard features AI-powered suggestions, instant language switching, and a privacy-first architecture that never stores keystrokes.

Feel free to use the above paragraph in articles. No approval needed.

Key features

Real-time translation: Type in one language, send in another. No app switching, no copy-paste.

100+ languages: From Korean and Japanese to Yoruba and Swahili — including underserved languages most keyboards ignore.

Works everywhere: Functions as a system keyboard in every iOS app — iMessage, WhatsApp, Instagram, email, and more.

Privacy-first: Zero keystrokes stored. Translations processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

Smart suggestions: AI-powered word predictions that improve with use, in every supported language.
