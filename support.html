Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about KeyLingo.

Getting started

How do I enable KeyLingo on my iPhone?
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Go to General → Keyboard → Keyboards
  3. Tap Add New Keyboard
  4. Select KeyLingo
  5. Tap KeyLingo again and enable Allow Full Access
Why does KeyLingo need "Allow Full Access"?

Full Access is required by iOS for any keyboard that connects to the internet. KeyLingo needs it to provide real-time translations between languages. We do NOT store, log, or share anything you type. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I switch to KeyLingo while typing?

While typing in any app, tap and hold the globe icon (🌐) on your keyboard and select KeyLingo from the list. You can also tap the globe icon to cycle through your keyboards.

Using KeyLingo

How do I translate text as I type?

Type your message in any language, then tap the translate button on the KeyLingo toolbar. Select the target language and your text will be converted instantly. Send the translated message as normal.

How do I switch between languages?

Tap the language selector on the KeyLingo toolbar to see all your available languages. Tap any language to switch to it. You can also set up favorite languages for quick access.

Which languages does KeyLingo support?

KeyLingo supports 100+ languages including English, Korean, Japanese, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Hindi, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Portuguese, Russian, Italian, Thai, Vietnamese, Swahili, Yoruba, and many more. We're constantly adding new languages.

Does KeyLingo work offline?

Basic keyboard typing works offline, but translation features require an internet connection to ensure accuracy.

Subscription & billing

What's included in the free version?

The free version includes basic keyboard functionality with up to 5 languages and a limited number of daily translations. Upgrade to premium for unlimited languages, unlimited translations, and priority speed.

How do I subscribe to premium?

Open the KeyLingo app and tap on the premium banner or go to Settings → Upgrade to Premium. Choose monthly or yearly and confirm with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select KeyLingo
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open the KeyLingo app and go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase the subscription.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your KeyLingo purchase.

Troubleshooting

KeyLingo isn't showing up as a keyboard option
  1. Make sure you've added KeyLingo in Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards
  2. Try restarting your iPhone
  3. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app, then add the keyboard again
Translations seem inaccurate

Translation accuracy depends on the language pair and context. For best results, type complete sentences rather than individual words. If you notice a consistent issue with a specific language, please report it to us so we can improve.

The keyboard is slow or laggy
  1. Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  2. Close other apps to free up memory
  3. Restart your iPhone
  4. Update to the latest version of KeyLingo
